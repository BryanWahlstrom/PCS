How do you add a newline to a String?

\n


What is the difference between single and double quotes?

Single quotes less flexible, can only escape quotes and backslashes. Double quotes allow you to put tabs and newlines and embed arbitrary expressions


How would you replace all instances of the word ‘pie’ with the word ‘pizza’ in a String?

string.gsub('pie', 'pizza')

What is the difference between gsub and gsub!?

gsub! will modify the string in place instead of returning a modified copy
