What does the dot character match? any single character

How do you match exactly 3 lower case characters? [a-z][a-z][a-z]

How do you match a range of characters? [0-9a-zA-Z_]

How do you match any whitespace? \s

How do you match any number? \d for each digit, also [0–9]*

How do you match either “ferret” or “weasel”? (ferret|weasel)

What’s the difference between * and +     the star searches for zero or any number of the character before it, the +  searches for one or more of the character before it

What does the operator =~ do in Ruby?  it tests whether the regexp matches a string

How do you match the beginning/end of a line? /\A  \z/
